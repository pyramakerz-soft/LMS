How to use this book?
             How to use this book?









                             This book takes students on an exciting journey through
                             storytelling, intertwining STEAM education with Sustainable
                             Development Goals (SDGs) to explore the food chain and its
                             vital role in ecosystems.

                             It illustrates how science, technology, engineering, art, and
                             math can deepen our understanding of energy
                             transformation in nature and the complex interconnections
                             that sustain life.


                             Emphasizing SDGs 13 ,12, and 15 (Responsible Consumption and
                             Production, Climate Action, and Life on Land), this journey
                             highlights how even small disruptions in the food chain, such
                             as the disappearance of a single species, can ripple across the
                             environment, a ecting biodiversity and ecosystem balance.


                             Students will encounter an interactive food chain simulation,
                             where each station represents di erent ecosystems-forests,
                             oceans, grasslands, and wetlands. Here, students will observe
                             how energy  ows from the sun to plants, then to herbivores,
                             carnivores, and decomposers, completing a circle that
                             supports life.

                             Through hands-on activities and storytelling, they’ll discover
                             the delicate balance of predator and prey, producer and
                             consumer, and how each organism plays an irreplaceable role
                             in sustaining the environment.

                             By linking ecological harmony with sustainable development,
                             this book invites students to reimagine their role in protecting
                             our planet.

                             Students will explore how our choices impact the food chain
                             and discover ways to safeguard the environment,
                             encouraging them to become stewards of a balanced,
                             thriving world for generations to come.
